PG 5e

Create and manage your character sheets for tabletop role-playing games (5e rules, SRD 5.1).

PG 5e is a web application, live and served over HTTPS, to build and keep your characters organized: stats, skills, saving throws, combat, spells, equipment and notes. It includes a built-in dice roller, tracking of hit points / spell slots / conditions, PDF export and read-only sharing of a sheet through a public link.

Plans and pricing

The service is free: every registered user can create 1 character sheet with all the in-game tools. A Premium Lifetime upgrade for €4.99 (one-time payment, no subscription) unlocks unlimited sheets and the customizable dashboard with a drag & drop sheet-layout editor. Web payments are handled by Paddle as the official reseller (Merchant of Record). Details on the pricing page.
